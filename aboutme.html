About Me


My name is Declan Whitmarsh. I was born and raised in Sydney Australia, but I moved to the United States as a teenager. I enjoy basketball, fishing, camping, and traveling. Last year I married my wife, Isabel, and we decided to move to Alaska for the summer to have our very own adventure. We both have dreams of making a positive difference in the world and we try everyday to find a way to work towards that goal. We are both passionate about non-profit work, and are officers in the pro-life club at BYU. Our next adventures are going to take us to Costa Rica, Japan, and then to Australia for Isabel's first time.

Some of my favorite places I have travelled include:

  1. Denali, Alaska

    • The Savage River
    • Savage Alpine

  2. Tasmania, Australia

    • Mt. Wellington by Hobart
    • The Gorge in Launceston

  3. Puerto Vallarta, Mexico

    • Any beach / taco shop
